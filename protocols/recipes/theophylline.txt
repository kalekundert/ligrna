30 mM theophylline
──────────────────
- 270.24 mg theophylline (Sigma T1633)
- milliQ water to 50 mL
- Stir and incubate at ≈85°C to dissolve

Note: theophylline precipitates after a few days, so check for thin, 
needle-like crystals before using.  If you see any, repeat the "stir and 
incubate" step above.
