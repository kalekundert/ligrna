1. Setup 5 restriction digest reactions by mixing
   the following reagents at room temperature in the 
   order given.

   Reagent               Conc  Each Rxn  Master Mix
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                        6.00 μL    33.00 μL
   CutSmart Buffer        10x   0.80 μL     4.40 μL
   EcoRI-HF           20 U/μL   0.10 μL     0.55 μL
   HindIII-HF         20 U/μL   0.10 μL     0.55 μL
   DNA              250 ng/μL   4.00 μL            
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
                               11.00 μL     7.00 μL/rxn

2. Incubate at 37°C for 1h, then at 80°C for 20 min 
   (to heat-inactivate the enzyme).

3. Setup the in vitro transcription reactions 
   immediately, or store at -20°C.
